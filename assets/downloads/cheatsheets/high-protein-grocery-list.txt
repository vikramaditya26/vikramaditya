THE SIMPLE GUY
High Protein Grocery List

Protein staples
- Eggs
- Curd / Greek yogurt
- Milk
- Paneer
- Tofu
- Soya chunks
- Chicken breast
- Whey protein

Easy carb sources
- Oats
- Rice
- Roti / atta
- Poha
- Potatoes
- Bananas

Healthy fat support
- Peanut butter
- Almonds
- Roasted peanuts
- Ghee in moderation

Low-effort backup snacks
- Fruit + curd
- Whey + banana
- Roasted chana
- Sprouts

Simple weekly rhythm
- Buy staples once a week.
- Restock fruit and curd mid-week.
- Keep 2 no-cook protein backups at all times.

Rule of thumb
- Make protein visible and easy.
- If it takes too much effort, you will skip it on busy days.
