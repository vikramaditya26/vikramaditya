THE SIMPLE GUY
Finance Basics Cheat Sheet

1. First priorities
- Know your monthly take-home.
- Know your fixed costs.
- Build an emergency fund before chasing higher returns.

2. Emergency fund target
- Minimum: 3 months of expenses.
- Better: 6 months.
- Keep it liquid, not locked away.

3. 50/30/20 starter rule
- 50% needs
- 30% wants
- 20% savings and investing

4. Debt rules
- Credit card debt is wealth destruction.
- High-interest debt should usually be attacked before investing aggressively.
- Avoid borrowing for vanity purchases.

5. Investing basics
- Start early.
- Stay consistent.
- Let compounding work.
- Index funds are enough for most beginners.

6. Insurance basics
- Health insurance matters.
- Term insurance only if someone depends on your income.
- Do not mix insurance and investing.

7. Money reminders
- Save first, spend second.
- Big fixed costs matter more than tiny daily guilt.
- A clean money system beats random intensity.
