THE SIMPLE GUY
Reading Notes Framework

Use these prompts after every reading session:

1. What idea stayed with me?
2. What line deserves to be copied exactly?
3. What did this challenge in my current thinking?
4. What should I do differently because of this?
5. Which page would I revisit one year later?

Simple note structure
- Book:
- Author:
- Why I picked it:
- Best quote:
- Three ideas:
- One disagreement:
- One action:
- Who should read it:

Before you re-read
- Did this book change behavior or only mood?
- Would I recommend it to a friend?
- Is there one chapter worth returning to?

Rule
- Notes are not decoration.
- If nothing changes after reading, the note is incomplete.
