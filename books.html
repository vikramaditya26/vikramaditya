Book Reviews

The Psychology of Money

Morgan Housel

This book explores how people think about money and why behavior often matters more than knowledge. It explains the importance of long-term thinking, the role of luck, and the danger of chasing quick wins. A must-read for building healthy financial habits.

Siddhartha

Hermann Hesse

A spiritual journey of self-discovery, this novel follows Siddhartha as he seeks meaning beyond material success. It blends wisdom, philosophy, and simplicity, teaching that true fulfillment comes from within. A timeless classic on life’s deeper questions.

Tao Te Ching

Lao Tzu

A foundational text of Taoist philosophy, this book emphasizes living in harmony with nature and simplicity. It offers short verses filled with wisdom on humility, balance, and flow. A guide for those seeking calmness and perspective in a noisy world.

Principles

Ray Dalio

Ray Dalio shares the principles that guided his life and business. The book breaks down decision-making, radical transparency, and learning from mistakes. It’s practical wisdom for building strong habits in both work and life.

The Almanack of Naval Ravikant

Eric Jorgenson

This collection of Naval Ravikant’s ideas covers wealth, happiness, and leverage. It emphasizes building specific knowledge, compounding relationships, and prioritizing freedom. A modern manual for thoughtful living and smart decision-making.

Poor Charlie’s Almanack

Charlie Munger

This book compiles the speeches and wisdom of Charlie Munger. It highlights mental models, rational thinking, and lifelong learning. A powerful guide for anyone who wants to think clearly and avoid common mistakes in life and investing.

Hsin Hsin Ming: The Book of Nothing

Osho

This book explores Zen wisdom, showing how to embrace nothingness and simplicity. It challenges overthinking and emphasizes direct experience of life. A deep yet accessible reflection on peace, silence, and freedom.

The Prophet

Kahlil Gibran

A poetic masterpiece, The Prophet reflects on love, work, freedom, and death. Each essay-like poem offers wisdom on living a meaningful life. A short but powerful read that inspires reflection and gratitude.

Atomic Habits

James Clear

This book explains how small habits compound into big changes over time. It gives practical strategies for building good habits, breaking bad ones, and designing your environment for success. A go-to guide for self-improvement.
