Why I Picked This

I like the harmonica because it feels portable, playful, and a little old-school. It is also intimidating in a very honest way: if your breathing is messy, the sound tells on you instantly.

This page is here so the challenge has a real home before the attempt. I will update it later with clips, rough notes, and the first melody I can play without thinking too hard.
