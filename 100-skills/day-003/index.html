What I Learned

This is placeholder guidance until the real attempt happens. The final version will include what stroke drills helped, how long I lasted before rushing, and what I would tell another beginner.

  • Slow, repeatable drills matter more than trying to write beautiful words too early.
  • Consistency in spacing is just as important as line variation.
  • It is a useful reminder that control usually looks simple from the outside.
